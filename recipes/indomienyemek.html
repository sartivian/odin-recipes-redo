Indomie Nyemek

Description

This is the best way to eat indomie Nyemek. It consist of noodles, protein and vegetables to its healthy and tasy

Ingridients

  • Indomie Goreng (whatever flavour)
  • Egg
  • Mustard Green
  • Onion
  • Hot Pepper
  • Tomato
  • Water
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Slice onion, hot pepper, and tomato
  2. Pour oil to the pan, and add the sliced ingridients
  3. Fry it until it smells good
  4. Add egg, mustard green to the pan and mix it
  5. Add indomie to the pan and pour water around 500 ml
  6. Add the indomie seasoing to the pan and additional seasoining as you like
  7. Cook it until the water on the pan is dried
  8. Finally,Pour it to a bowl and it's ready
