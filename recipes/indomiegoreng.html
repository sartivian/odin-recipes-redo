Indomie Goreng

Description

This is the best way to eat indomie Goreng. It consist of noodles, protein and vegetables to its healthy and tasy

Ingridients

  • Indomie Goreng (whatever flavour)
  • Egg
  • Mustard Green
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pour water on pan around 500 ml
  2. Put the pan on the stove and boil the water
  3. After it boils, crack egg and put in on the water
  4. Wait for around 5 minutes until the egg is cooked
  5. Put the indomie into the pan until it cooked
  6. Put the Mustard green finto the pan or a minute
  7. Drain the broth from the pan
  8. Put the noodles, egg and mustard greens to the bowl
  9. FInally, add the seasoning to it
